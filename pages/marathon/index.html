Marathon

For runners aiming to achieve a sub-4:30 marathon time, here is a 16-week training plan for marathon preparation.

In order to achieve a marathon time of 4 hours and 30 minutes, you'll need to maintain an average pace of around 10 minutes per mile throughout the entire race. To finish faster than 4:30, you should also be able to complete a half-marathon in under 2 hours and a 10K in under 53 minutes. It's recommended to have a regular running routine of 20-30 minutes, four or five times a week, before beginning this plan.
